# Flag Trivia

## Project Summary

**Flag Trivia** is a fully on-chain flag quiz game built with Dojo Engine. Players connect their Cartridge Controller wallet and test their knowledge of world flags, with every score recorded on-chain. Compete against other players on a live on-chain leaderboard to claim the top spot.

## How to Play

1. Connect your wallet via Cartridge Controller
2. A flag is displayed — pick the correct country from the multiple choice options
3. Answer as many flags correctly as you can
4. Your score is saved on-chain — check the leaderboard to see your rank
