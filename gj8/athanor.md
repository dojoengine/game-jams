# Athanor

## Project Summary

**Athanor** is a fully on-chain competitive grimoire race built on Starknet using the Dojo engine.

Players send heroes on expeditions through dangerous zones, gather rare ingredients, craft potions by trial-and-error, and race to discover all recipes in their grimoire. The first to complete the grimoire wins.

**Core loop**: Explore > Gather > Craft > Buff heroes > Explore deeper > Discover all 10 recipes.

Features:
- 3 exploration zones with increasing danger and rarer ingredients
- 3 recruitable heroes with persistent stats (HP, power, regen)
- 36 possible ingredient combinations, 10 secret recipes per game
- Real-time expedition simulation computed on-chain with Cartridge VRF
- Potion crafting system with progressive discovery and hint purchasing
- Competitive leaderboard ranked by fastest grimoire completion
- Soulbound NFT game sessions via Provable Games embeddable game standard
- Phaser 3 visual exploration with particle effects and animated events
- Cartridge Controller with session keys for gasless play

## How to Play

1. Connect with Cartridge Controller
2. Click "New Game" to mint a game session and start
3. Send a hero on an expedition into one of 3 zones (Verdant Meadow, Crystal Cavern, Aether Spire)
4. Wait for the hero to return, then claim your loot (ingredients + gold)
5. Combine two ingredients to try to discover a recipe - success creates a potion, failure gives you a clue
6. Apply potions to heroes to permanently buff their stats (HP, power, regen)
7. Recruit additional heroes with gold (up to 3)
8. Buy hints with gold if you're stuck
9. Discover all 10 recipes to complete your grimoire and win!
