# Game Jam VI

**Date:** June 27-29, 2025

**Prize Pool:** $25,000

**Submissions:** 23 projects from 143 builders

**Winners Announcement:** https://x.com/ohayo_dojo/status/1940062907355865475

## Winners

### First Place ($5,000 each)

**Moonbag**
Cosmic push-your-luck roguelike where every tap could mean moon rocks or devastation.
Draw orbs from the bag for points, avoid bombs that end your run, shop for power-ups between rounds.

**Overgoal**
Rise from street footballer to legend.
Arcade-style match sequences with off-field decisions that affect stats—endorsements, nightlife, training.
Full on-chain player history.

### Second Place ($2,500 each)

**Craft Island Explorer's Map**
Mod that transforms island exploration with procedurally generated islands, fog-of-war resource gathering, and mobile controls.

**Panda Panda**
Browser-based puzzle game inspired by Sheep a Sheep.
Strategic tile matching with limited slot management and increasing difficulty.

**Ashwood**
Medieval warfare on a 54-position battlefield.
Seasonal combat mechanics, supply chain logistics, 6 unique unit classes.

**You Are Who You Kill**
Frankenstein-style combat where you build from your enemies.
Collect body parts from defeated foes, each granting unique stats.

**Caps**
Chess reimagined with special abilities and AI opponents.
4 teams with unique playstyles, ELO-based matchmaking, energy-based special moves.

**Elven Escape**
3D adventure through mystical forests.
Ancient shrines, formidable gatekeepers, deadly trap navigation—mobile-first 3D experience.
