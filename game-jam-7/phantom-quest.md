# Phantom Quest

### Submission Track
Full game

### Project Summary
A turn-based dungeon crawler where players control a party of spectral heroes navigating procedurally generated dungeons. Battle enemies, collect loot, and level up your phantoms using on-chain randomness for fair item drops and combat outcomes.

Demo: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
