# Take Your Shot

### Project Summary
> Give a short summary of your game project.
Simple 3D first person shooter, connected to a vesu liquidation bot, allows the player to shoot in a random generated maze against an increasingly faster enemy, when the player dies the liquidation bot gets a number of liquidation earning assigned to the user's wallet, based on the number of killed enemies.
