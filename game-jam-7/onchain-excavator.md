### Project Summary

> Give a short summary of your game project.

Onchain Excavator is a treasure-mining strategy game built with Dojo on Starknet.
Players compete to excavate digital mines, uncover hidden treasures, and earn onchain rewards.
Every action from digging to claiming a find is recorded on the blockchain, proving who truly rules the mines.
