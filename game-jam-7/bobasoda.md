### Project Summary
> BobaSoda is a first of it's kind fast paced tiktok/bumble themed prediction market game where players can get onboarded and start betting on real time 60s bet rounds on whether a token price is going to ho up or down from the last round, based on their prediction they can earn payouts varying from 1x to as much as 8x or higher.

The key innovation lies in the concept and the UI/UX:
BobaSoda is ready to play from mobile devices and the unique bumble/tiktok format enables players to interact with the interface fast and and makes the platform addictive.

Future milestones:
Integration of 15+ tokens and a real time AI mindshare page

How cartridge was useful here:
The game is all about being fast, and using cartridge's embedded wallet was a no brainer for us at the same time dojo engine gave us provable fairness since the game involves betting with money.
